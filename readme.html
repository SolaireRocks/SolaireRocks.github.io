Thanks for downloading this patch!

6. Bug Fixes and Explanations　
▲　

Original Game Bug Fixes

Note: The vast majority of bugs in FF6 have been fixed in general; only a few remain that unless you know this game in and out/are a speedrunner you will most likely never encounter them. Otherwise, the ones that remain won't affect gameplay and the list below are just ones Tsushiy made note of. In general, 99% of what you can think of has been fixed -Kain.

  • Vanish Magic Bug
    Originally if you used the spell Vanish then another magic spell on the target, it would ALWAYS work, especially with X-Zone. This has been corrected and resistances now work properly.
  • Evasion Bug
    In the original game Evasion checked Magic Evasion. This has been fixed.
  • Blind Bug
    Originally Blind had no effect what-so-ever. This has now been corrected.
  • Shadow Leaves the Party - Party Member Change Bug
    In the original game if you took a party of Gau only to recruit Shadow when finding Terra and then went to the Veldt, used Leap with Gau and then returned to Narshe, Shadow would leave and crash the game with an empty party. This has been fixed because Gau no longer has Leap in this patch.
  • Damage Overflow Bug
    In the original game making your statuses extremely high with Eidolon leveling and equipment would cause spell damage to overflow and do pitiful damage (Celes doing Blizzard for 30 damage as an example). This has been fixed because stats no longer change on level up now.
  • Airship Bug
    Upon arriving on the Floating Continent the game still counts you as on the airship. If you leave, then immediately return and die, it puts you on the airsip and changes a value. This can cause bugs and allows for interesting things to happen. However, this has been changed because now you're returned to the Title Screen upon death.
  • Psycho Cyan Bug
    In the original game when Cyan would use Sky, then die and be revived, the flag for it would never wear off, thus allowing for constant free counter attacks. This has been fixed.
  • Psycho Cyan Imp Bug
    Like the above bug expect if Cyan is made into an Imp, he will now attack forever until the enemy is dead. This can't be fully fixed, however he will now only counter attack once.

7. Frequently Asked Questions　
▲　

Q. Where's the Eidolon that teaches ○? or ○? How do you learn the magic ●? or ●? (Note: These are blank on purpose for examples.)
A. Many things have been deleted, changed, or added. Please see the html files for more information.

Q. I can't beat this boss...
A. Final Fantasy 6 is a game with a large level correction. Gain some levels and try again.

Q. The enemy attacks so fast and I act so slow.
A. More powerful actions such as "Blitz", "Tools", and "X-Magic" take longer to cast due to their power. Try using "Haste", "Slow", etc. "Fight" and using Eidolons are also very quick actions.

Q. Random encounters are way too difficult.
A. You'll run into problems if you have the mentality from the original game of just using Fight only.

Enemies with complete status immunity are rare, including bosses. I can't stress this enough. Try using Confuse, Blind, Sleep, Silence, Toad, etc, even if you think "there's no way that'll work!", you may be surprised...

Also, unlike the original game, instant death, petrification resistance, and near-death tolerance are now separated. Break, Gravity, and Tornado may work on enemies they previously didn't. Death is also an option now.

Q. I can't fight Valigarmanda in the World of Ruin.
A. Terra must be in your party now. Try again.

Q. I can't beat the new battle against Kefka on the Floating Continent.
A. Even if you lose, the game will proceed like normal. However, if you win, you'll get a nice item. He has a very nice item to steal, too!

Q. The random encounters underneath Figaro Castle in the World of Ruin are too difficult, and there's no way to recover...
A. The collapsed soldier at the start of the dungeon will now freely heal you.

Q. Yellow and Skull Dragon are no longer in Kefka's Tower?
A. I've moved them to new places. Try exploring areas you've visited previously.

Q. I can't open the king's room at Doma Castle once inside Cyan's Dream. Why?
A. Walk around and view all of his past memories, then you'll be able to.

Q. How do you win the last 3 solo battles of Cyan's Dream?
A. Even if you lose, the game will proceed like normal, but you'll get a nice accessory for winning.

Q. How can I defeat the Holy Dragon in Fanatic's Tower?
A. Defeat "Omniscient" (すべてをしるもの) at the very top, then the battle against Holy Dragon will be normalized. (Fight, etc will become available.)

Q. I keep hearing odd noises during some of the BGM...
A. It's the emulator. I use UOSNES Version 20100527, as that what was checked and used for the patch, so I highly recommend you use it as well for the best performance. (We've attempted and had most of the glaring song issues fixed thanks to emberling in the English version, but some are still off slightly. Please listen to the music on UOSNES if you want the best version. The reason for the sound quality is due to UOSNES bypassing the SNES audio RAM limitation, where ZSNES, 9X, etc, all have limits. The sound quality is superior and is not possible to truly fix for any other emulator beyond fixing the beeps and boops or grating glitchy noises.)
