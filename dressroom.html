Terra (ティナ)





Silk Dress: Hybrid
《 シルクのドレス : ハーフ 》

This costume is obtained in a treasure box in Maduin's House in the Eidolon World during the World of Ruin.

It's a dress her mother, Madeline, made.

The BGM played in the Falcon while wearing this costume is "Romancing Saga 3: Catalina's Theme".





Bodhum Military Uniform: l'Cie
《 ボーダム治安連隊軍服：ルシ 》

To obtain this costume you must have completed her personal achievement "The Feeling of Love".

The appearance used here is Lightning's model from Final Fantasy XIII.

The BGM played in the Falcon while wearing this costume is "Final Fantasy XIII: March of the Dreadnoughts"





Flower Seller's Dress: Cetra
《 花売りのワンピース：古代種 》

This costume is obtained once you've cleared the achievement "Character Master".

The costume is designed after Aerith's dress from Final Fantasy VII.

The BGM played in the Falcon while wearing this costume is "Final Fantasy VII: Aerith's Theme".





Feymarch Bustier: Summoner
《 幻界のビスチェ：魔界幻士 》

To obtain this costume you must have unlocked the first 3 costumes for Terra and cleared the "Quest Achiever" achievement.

This costume is designed from Rydia's dress in Final Fantasy IV.

The BGM played in the Falcon while wearing this costume is "Final Fantasy IV: Rydia".
